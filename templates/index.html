How Stock Tokenization Works

Stock tokens represent fractional ownership in a company's shares, allowing investors to buy a portion of expensive stocks.

  1. We divide each stock into multiple tokens
  2. You purchase tokens at a fraction of the full stock price
  3. Token values change in real-time with the stock price
  4. Buy and sell tokens anytime during market hours
